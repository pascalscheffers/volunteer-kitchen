# Equipment

Known, confirmed kitchen kit for Bornhack 2026 — split by what the venue
provides and what people are bringing from home. This is the *confirmed*
inventory; gear we're still weighing up to rent or borrow is tracked separately
in the planning notes, not here.

## Available on site (provided)

- **4× burners** — each handles a 15 L pot.
- **1× professional convection oven** — 6 trays.
- **Large food processor** + **stick blender**.
- **Stand mixer with dough hook** (in addition to Pascal's Ankarsrum below).
- **Commercial hood/conveyor dishwasher.**
- **Walk-in cooler** + **commercial fridge** + **freezer**.

## Brought by members

### Pascal

- **Ankarsrum stand mixer** — heavy-duty Swedish mixer, comfortable with ~5 kg
  of dough per batch. Carries the pita day (~150 pieces) and the big focaccia /
  brioche runs.
- **Sous-vide immersion circulator** — 1 confirmed. A second unit may be
  borrowed (outcome TBC); the plan defaults to a single-circulator schedule.
- **Yeti Tundra 35 cooler** — dual-role: cold-chain transport for perishable
  pre-prep on arrival, then repurposed as the insulated sous-vide vessel. Holds
  bath temperature with minimal heater duty cycle.
- **Clamp-style vacuum sealer** — good quality; covers all sous-vide and
  freeze-ahead vac-packing needs.
- **Breville Smart Grinder Pro** — coffee grinder; ~2.4 kg/day capacity, ~1 h of
  grinding per morning for ~75 person-days.

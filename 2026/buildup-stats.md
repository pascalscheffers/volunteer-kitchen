## We baked our own bread 🍞

- **2-3 batches of 5 kg dough, every day**
- **~60 kg of dough** over the build → roughly **75 loaves**
- Ate through **37 of 44 kg** of flour doing it (7 kg left standing)

## Fun-size facts

- **~20 kg of sugar** dissolved into cakes, sauces and coffee.
- **~170 litres** of soda, juice and squash drunk — plus coffee by the kilo.
- **~520 g of raw ingredients per person, per day** — before water, before
  cooking. Portions ran ~900 g/dinner early, settling to ~650 g as the group
  normalised.
- Biggest single line item: **chicken fillet, 14 kg** for 1,085 DKK.

## The waste & the leftovers

- **~20% of veg binned** — roughly **11 kg**, about **620 DKK** into the compost.
- Walked away at the end with **7 kg flour, 3 kg meat, 3 kg cheese** to spare.

Yes, it is a shit tonne of food. You really do need more than you think.

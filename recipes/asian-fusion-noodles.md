For these vegetables, I would go for a flavour profile that is fresh, umami-rich, and slightly sweet. Here are a couple of options:

Classic Asian

Soy sauce
Sesame oil
Fresh ginger
Garlic
A little honey or maple syrup
Rice vinegar or lime juice
Finish with sesame seeds and spring onions

Thai-inspired

Red curry paste
Coconut milk (if you’d like the dish to be creamy)
Lime
Soy sauce
Fresh coriander (cilantro)
Chilli to taste
